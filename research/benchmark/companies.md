## Benchmark Notes

- Keep one row per material company/aircraft update.
- Do not restate unchanged rows from earlier research.
- Prefer official company releases, investor materials, regulator documents, type certificate records, production certificate records, and credible primary technical materials.
- Use concise but comparable wording in `主要性能`, such as range, speed, payload, passenger count, propulsion type, and noise where available.
- Use `主なリスク` for certification uncertainty, funding runway, production readiness, battery/thermal limits, autonomy limits, infrastructure dependency, or commercial adoption risk.
